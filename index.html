How it works

Share your plans once — we handle the rest.

No more juggling dozens of tabs or guessing which itinerary will actually work. With Empire Concierge USA, you send your details once, and we respond with options that are realistic, clear and ready to book.

  1. Tell us about your trip.
    Departure city, destination(s), dates or date range, cabin preference, who is traveling and any special notes (dietary, cultural, mobility, celebration, etc.).
  2. We research and curate options.
    We compare airlines, schedules and hotels, then send a focused list of flight and hotel combinations — not pages of random results.
  3. Confirm, book and travel with support.
    Once you choose your preferred plan, we handle ticketing and reservations. You travel knowing someone familiar with your itinerary is available to help.
Good to know

• We work with both business and personal trips — many clients use us for both.
• If something is not realistic for your dates or budget, we’ll say so and suggest alternatives instead of forcing a poor fit.
• You’re not locked in by asking for options; use us as your travel desk when it makes sense.

Not sure where to start? A simple message with your from / to, approximate dates and how you like to travel is enough to begin.
