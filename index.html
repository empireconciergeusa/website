How it works

Simple, personal and fully concierge-managed.

You don’t need to hunt across dozens of sites or worry about tiny details. Share your plans once – Empire Concierge USA builds, refines and confirms an itinerary that fits your schedule, preferences and budget.

  1. Share your trip details. Send us your routes, dates, cabin preference (economy, business, first), hotel expectations and any special notes – flexibility, Shabbos, kids, simchas and more.
  2. We research smart options. We compare airlines, schedules and properties, then present curated options that balance comfort, timing and value – never just the cheapest random fare.
  3. Confirm & travel with support. Once you approve the itinerary, we finalize tickets and reservations, then remain available for questions, adjustments and travel-day support.
